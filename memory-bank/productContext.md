# Product context

## Why
Codryn needs a professional web presence to showcase real client/team work across web, mobile, and AI products and to convert visitors into inquiries.

## How it should work
Visitors land on a brand-led hero, browse selected projects with demos/live links, understand services, and contact Codryn via form or direct email/phone.

## UX goals
- Fast, clean, trustworthy developer aesthetic (Linear / Vercel / Supabase feel)
- Clear hierarchy: brand → work → contact
- Responsive on mobile, tablet, desktop
- Subtle motion without distraction
